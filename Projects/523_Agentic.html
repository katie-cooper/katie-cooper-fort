Designing and deploying an AI-powered agent to assist UNC faculty, with future scalability in mind.

Role: Project Lead

Fall 2025

About the Project

Our team is designing a product for our class BOLD 523: Software Engineering Lab. We are partnering with our client, Kris Jordan, a faculty member in the Department of Computer Science at UNC. Our goal is to create an agentic AI agent capable of grading students' projects based on a given rubric. This tool will enable teachers to efficiently assign and grade open-ended, creative, and cutting-edge projects, supporting innovative learning experiences in their classes.

Key Features

  • Developing a conversational AI agent capable of integrating with faculty workflows
  • Designing modular architecture for customization and scalability across different clients
  • Implementing intelligent task management and natural language understanding for user queries
  • Conducting iterative client feedback sessions to ensure usability and alignment with teaching goals
